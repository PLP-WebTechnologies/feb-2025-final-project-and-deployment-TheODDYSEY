about us

more about Kenya Nature Blog!

Welcome to our Kenya Nature Blog! Here, we celebrate the beauty and wonders of Kenya's natural world. From the lush Kakamega Forest to the serene shores of Lake Victoria, we aim to inspire a deeper connection with Kenya's diverse ecosystems. Explore our stories, tips, and guides to help you appreciate and protect Kenya's environment. Together, we can make a difference for our beautiful country.

Join us as we delve into topics like sustainable living in Kenya, wildlife conservation in the Maasai Mara and Amboseli, and the healing power of Kenya's natural landscapes. Whether you're an adventurer exploring Mount Kenya, a nature lover visiting our national parks, or someone looking to make eco-friendly changes in your Kenyan lifestyle, there's something here for everyone. Let's embark on this journey to cherish and preserve Kenya's natural heritage!

Our Mission

To inspire Kenyans and visitors to connect with Kenya's natural wonders, promote sustainable living practices in our communities, and raise awareness about the importance of preserving Kenya's biodiversity for future generations.

Our Vision

We envision a Kenya where citizens live in harmony with nature, cherishing the beauty of our national parks and forests, and protecting our natural resources for a sustainable future that benefits all Kenyans.

Kenyan Forest Conservation

Learn about the importance of Kenya's forests like Kakamega, Mount Kenya, and the Aberdares, and how you can contribute to their preservation. These forests are vital for Kenya's biodiversity and climate regulation.

Kenyan Wildlife Protection

Discover the efforts being made to protect Kenya's endangered species like elephants, rhinos, and lions, and how you can help safeguard wildlife habitats in Kenya's national parks and reserves.

Sustainable Living in Kenya

Explore tips and ideas for adopting a sustainable lifestyle in Kenya, from reducing waste to using locally-made eco-friendly products in your daily life, supporting Kenyan green initiatives.

Kenyan Eco-Tourism

Find out how to travel responsibly across Kenya and enjoy our natural wonders without harming the environment. Support local Kenyan communities and protect our diverse ecosystems from the coast to the highlands.

Kenyan Nature Photography

Capture the beauty of Kenya's natural world through photography. Learn tips for taking stunning shots of Kenya's landscapes, wildlife in the Maasai Mara, and the Great Rift Valley.

Climate Action in Kenya

Understand the impact of climate change on Kenya's ecosystems and how you can take action to reduce your carbon footprint and support Kenyan environmental initiatives and conservation efforts.

Kenyan Community Involvement

Join hands with like-minded Kenyans and organizations to participate in activities like tree planting in the Mau Forest, beach cleanups along the Kenyan coast, and environmental awareness campaigns in local communities.
